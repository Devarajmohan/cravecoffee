HOW TO MAKE A COFFEE

Enjoy a perfect cup of coffee made effortlessly

  • Step-1

    Heat water it reaches just below boiling around 200°F or 93°C.

  • Step-2

    Grind fresh coffee beans to a medium coarseness.

  • Step-3

    Place the coffee grounds in a filter or coffee maker and pour hot water evenly over them.

  • Step-4

    Allow the coffee to steep or brew for about 4 minutes to extract flavors.

  • Step-5

    Pour the brewed coffee into your favorite mug and enjoy your freshly made cup of coffee!
